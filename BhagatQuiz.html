Bhagat Singh Quiz

Q-1 When was Bhagat Singh born?

Bhagat Singh was born into a Punjabi Sikh family on 27 September 1907[1] in the village of Banga in the Lyallpur district of the Punjab in what was then British India and is today Pakistan; he was the second of seven children—four sons, and three daughters—born to Vidyavati and her husband Kishan Singh Sandhu

Q-2 What was Bhagat Singh’s childhood nickname?

Bhagat Singh's Childhood Nickname was rangila. which was given by his mother to him and later was caled by everyone.

Q-3 Which incident deeply influenced Bhagat Singh’s political beliefs and actions

He was particularly affected by the Jallianwala Bagh massacre (1919) and the violence against unarmed Akali protestors at Nankana Sahib.

Q-4 Bhagat Singh was executed in which of the following case?

Bhagat Singh and his associates, Rajguru and Sukhdev were hanged to death in connection with the Lahore Conspiracy Case.

Q-5 When was Bhagat Singh executed?

Bhagat Singh was executed on 23 March 1931 in Lahore Conspiracy Case at 7:30 pm

Q-6 Bhagat Singh was executed at the age of ____.

Bhagat Singh was executed at the age of 23 years. His early execution made him a national hero of the Indian freedom struggle against colonial rule.

Q-7 An 18-foot tall bronze statue of Bhagat Singh was installed in ______.

An 18-foot tall bronze statue of Bhagat Singh was installed in the Parliament of India on 15 August 2008. His statue was installed next to the statues of former Prime Minister of India Indira Gandhi and Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose. .
